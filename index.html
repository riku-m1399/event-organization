About

About me

I'm a hiphop dancer. I've been dancing since 2007. I do dance showcases and dance battles. I live in Osaka and I belong to "The Soul Providers (TSP)".
As a leader of the dance team of TSP, I've been organizing some dance events. Sometimes I also do DJ in my events.

About TSP

TSP is an artists' collective in Osaka composed of dancers, DJs, beatmakers, photographers, etc. TSP organizes some music events such as party, music live, dance session, dance battle, etc.
